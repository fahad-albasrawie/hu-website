Exploring Earth, Preserving the Future: A Journey with Geoscience & Environment

Welcome to the Faculty of Geosciences and the environment at Hormuud University.
The Faculty of Geosciences and the Environment is interested in the natural and/or anthropogenic modifications of our planet, and more particularly in the Alpine areas and urban metropolises. The consequences for humans, animal, and plant species are also part of his research fields. The disciplines of geosciences and the environment are developing at the border between exact, experimental, and human sciences; they are in essence transdisciplinary.

Embark on a journey of discovery and stewardship with the Faculty of Geoscience & Environment, where we delve into the mysteries of our planet and strive to protect its invaluable resources for future generations. Our programs offer a multidisciplinary approach to understanding the Earth, from its deepest oceans to its highest peaks, and the complex environmental systems that sustain life.Embrace the opportunity to work alongside experts in state-of-the-art facilities and research centers, where you'll explore the complexities of agro-ecosystems, plant and animal biotechnology, and sustainable agricultural practices. Our commitment to innovation extends beyond the classroom, encouraging you to engage in groundbreaking research that addresses the pressing challenges of our time: climate change, food scarcity, and biodiversity loss.

Through innovative research and hands-on fieldwork, you'll gain the knowledge and skills needed to address some of the most pressing issues facing our world today: climate change, natural resource management, and environmental sustainability. Our faculty is dedicated to fostering a deep appreciation for the natural world and the importance of scientific inquiry in solving environmental challenges.

As part of our community, you'll have the opportunity to work alongside leading scientists in state-of-the-art facilities, engage in transformative research projects, and contribute to global efforts to ensure a sustainable future for our planet.

The faculty currently offers the following degree programmes:

  • Geology
  • Environmental Science

Join us in our mission to explore Earth and preserve its beauty and biodiversity for generations to come. Together, we can make a difference in understanding our world and safeguarding its treasures.
