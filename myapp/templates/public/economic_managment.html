Welcome to the Faculty of Economics and Management Sciences (FEMS), at Hormuud University. The FEMS is one of the developer Faculties at Hormuud University.

The Faculty aims to continuously strengthen its position as the leading institution in the fields of Economics, Financial and Management Sciences through its academic and research excellence. At the same time the Faculty remains committed to delivering competent, creative, responsible and productive citizens (Thought Leaders) of Hormuud University.

Embark on a transformative journey at the Faculty of Economics & Management Sciences, where we cultivate leaders poised to navigate the complexities of the global economy. In a world defined by rapid change and endless possibilities, our programs offer a blend of theoretical depth and practical insight, preparing you to make meaningful impacts in business, finance, and beyond.

Our curriculum is designed to challenge and inspire, fostering a deep understanding of economic principles, management strategies, and the dynamics of the marketplace. Through a combination of rigorous coursework, real-world case studies, and collaborative projects, you'll develop the analytical skills, strategic thinking, and innovative vision required to lead in the 21st century.

The faculty currently offers the following degree programmes:

  • Islamic Banking and Finance
  • Logistics and Procurement
  • Business Administration
  • Public Administration
  • Islamic Economics
  • Accounting
