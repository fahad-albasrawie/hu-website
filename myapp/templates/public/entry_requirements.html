Overview

Hormuud University welcomes your application! This guide outlines the key steps and timelines to ensure a smooth journey. Review your eligibility (certificates, letter, fee) and choose your program before submitting your application. Prepare for the program-specific entry exam after the deadline. Upon passing, your application will be reviewed, and you'll receive the admission decision shortly. For detailed information and forms, visit the "Apply Now" section on the university website. Remember, deadlines and specifics might change, so check the official website for the latest updates. Good luck with your application!

Application process

  • Ensure your application is complete. We cannot review it until everything is submitted.
  • Choose your program carefully. This is an important decision that will impact your studies.
  • Pay the $30 admission fee to one of the following bank accounts:
    • SALAAM SOMALI BANK: 30028742
    • Note: The application fee is non-refundable.
  • After submitting your application, we will contact you (email or call) regarding your entry or acceptance exam.
