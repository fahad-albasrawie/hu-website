Faculty Deans' Message to Students, Navigating Your Academic Path

Faculty of Engineering

Unveil Innovation Strategies for Your Pillowside
Eng. Mohamed Said Yusuf
Dean, Faculty of Engineering, Hormuud University

Welcome to the Faculty of Engineering at Hormuud University. We feel proud that among all private universities in Somalia we are the pioneer in establishing the graduate programs in Telecommunication, Mechanical, and Computer Engineering...

Charting Your Course towards Technological Advancement

Students enrolled in our programs take balance set of modules to obtain a solid foundation in engineering and general education. Our Students undergo industrial and vacation training to acquire and sharpen their technical and communication skills.

As well as the professional approach of the Faculty, Alhamdulillah, here at Hormuud University, we are proud to offer a high-quality education and friendly environment where the students can generate creative ideas, develop the teamwork attitude and leadership skills needed for their professional careers.

Our programs develop the knowledge and professional skills of our students, for careers that lead the future development of Electronics, Communications, Mechanical, Construction and Computing technologies.

The university has developed numerous lab facilities for the students, which includes Communication lab, Computer lab, Internet and simulation lab,Electronics lab, Circuit, and measurement lab, Microprocessor lab, Physics lab.

Highly qualified academic members are leading the faculty (most of them completed their higher education from renowned universities in the world).

The faculty currently offers the following degree programs:

  • Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering
  • Bachelor of Science in Telecommunication Engineering
  • Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering
  • Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering
  • Bachelor of Science in Engineering Geology
  • Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering

Besides that, the faculty arranges various activities like seminar, workshop, view exchange programs, science fair, sports tournaments, and technology contest etc. that will enhance to build up student’s quality beyond the academic syllabus.

Eng. Mohamed Said Yusuf
Dean, Faculty of Engineering
Hormuud University

The faculty of computer science and information technology of Hormuud University thrives the educational background that enables the students of sciences and technology to gain the required knowledge and experience through which they will be able to work any field related to computer science and IT...

Unveiling the Digital World and Navigating the Landscape of Information Technology

Even though the computer science and IT engineering are very challenging in Somalia we have being able to become one of the top leading universities in these fields through faculty instructors who have the required academic experience of teaching to deliver the best practices and to achieve the maximum objectives, this practices can be seen from the computer science and IT labs that are busy with students performing programs in different fields on science and IT to practice the materials being thought in the class with the help of the lab engineers that are ready to fulfill the student needs. We have very rich library full of computer science and IT books to make sure the students have the best reference books to flow.

Our development and research center newly brought highly trained professionals specialized for CISCO certification to build the networking base of our students to prepare them to CISCO CERTIFICATION exams.

Our training department together with the research and development center arranges quality-training section which covers variety of areas like web development using (HTML 5), IOS App development, Graphic Designing using (PS&AI), C++ programing, JAVA programing, CISCO CCNA, AI, Data science etc.

By providing a diverse range of contents in our program, we are trying to make sure that our students are fit for various career opportunities, all of which are exciting, rewarding, and greatly needed for our rapidly developing country. Depending upon their choice, they may either join field jobs, such as Software Engineers, Sales Managers, Hardware and Software Vendors, Consultants, Programmers, etc., or work in academic institutions as researchers or teachers.

My message to students will be “if you didn’t stand for something you will fall for everything”, as you join us, we will lead you to the right path of becoming one of the best programmers and app developers in the country, not only we are giving you a knowledge required to this field we will also help you reach your potential by consultancy and discussion. As you enroll to this department, we are expecting you to utilize every hour, every minute and every second of the four years you are spending with us and never skip a class/lecture because if you do you will never catch up.

It is very highly recommended for the students to attend all the classes and section to be able to pass or attend the final exams as the attendance has very serious regulations in our department and anyone who violates it will be sanctioned accordingly. we are defined by our values and social welfare but we are under no circumstances except or tolerate any misconduct against the university rule-of-law, remember This is not our only department to make it the best in the country but it is your department as well so we need you to help us to develop it together for farther reach. Please allow me to welcome you again to the one of the fastest growing and most job demanding faculties in the world.

The faculty currently offers the following degree programs:

  • Bachelor of Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Information Technology
  • Bachelor of Data Science
  • Bachelor of Software Engineering

The program aims to equip students for a range of career opportunities, including technical and academic roles. The university emphasizes the importance of consistent attendance and engagement in classes, as this is crucial for success and compliance with strict attendance policies. Misconduct is not tolerated, aligning with the university's values and rules. The faculty welcomes new students to this fast-growing and in-demand field, encouraging them to contribute to its development.

Faculty of Agriculture

Harvesting Knowledge and Skills
Eng Yusuf Ali Arif.
Dean, Faculty of Agriculture.

Welcome to the faculty of agriculture at Hormuud University. Generally, agriculture plays a significant role in the economy and prosperity of the world. Especially, in Somalia one of the main natural resources of the country is agriculture....

Join the Faculty of Agriculture at Hormuud University for a Fruitful Future

According to the study programs; the faculty gives students full education about this faculty, practical lessons, workshops and seminars, and training about agriculture. The faculty has a field required for students’ practical program and to research anything about agriculture. The faculty also has fully implemented greenhouse fields, small tools, and machines that facilitate to practice students and easy to do field operations.

As faculty of Agriculture at Hormuud University, our vision and mission are to produce well-qualified and skilled full agriculturists (students) that have the ability to work in fields for production and in the academic arena for education. Finally, every person, parent or student who needs his/ his children’s agricultural ambition to become true welcome to the faculty of agriculture.

This faculty comprise four departments:

  • Department of Agronomy (Soil, crop & environmental study)
  • Department of Plant protection (Generally plant pathology & plant entomology)
  • Department of Horticulture (Fruit, vegetable, ornamental plants & land scaping agriculture)
  • Department of Animal Husbandry (Generally animal production & animal medicine)

Eng Yusuf Ali Arif, the Dean of the Faculty of Agriculture at Hormuud University, extends a warm welcome to those interested in the vital field of agriculture, which is especially crucial in Somalia. The faculty is composed of four departments: Agronomy, Plant Protection, Horticulture, and Animal Husbandry, each focusing on different aspects of agriculture, from soil and crop studies to animal production and medicine.

Eng Yusuf Ali Arif.
Dean, Faculty of Agriculture.
