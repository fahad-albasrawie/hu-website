Dear Students, Parents, and University Stakeholders,
The Finance Office of Hormuud University is pleased to share an important update regarding our tuition fee structure. In line with our continued efforts to enhance access to higher education, we have introduced a revised and more flexible tuition policy designed to ease financial pressure while upholding academic excellence.

As part of Hormuud University’s vision to deliver inclusive, high-quality education that empowers individuals and contributes to national development, we are committed to removing barriers to enrollment and retention, ensuring that every student—regardless of background—has the opportunity to pursue their academic and professional aspirations in a supportive environment. Aligned with this mission, the Finance Office is dedicated to promoting financial transparency and accountability, ensuring efficient and effective resource management, supporting sustainable financial planning, and offering student-centered financial solutions that enhance access and encourage retention.

As the department responsible for managing the university’s financial operations—including budgeting, accounting, payroll, planning, and reporting—the Finance Office remains committed to upholding transparency, eDfficiency, and strict compliance with all institutional policies to support the university’s overall goals.

Together, we can build a stronger, more accessible future for higher education.
