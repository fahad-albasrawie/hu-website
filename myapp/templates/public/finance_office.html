Abdullahi

Finance Office, Hormuud University

The finance office is a department responsible for managing the financial operations of the university includes budgeting, accounting, financial reporting, payroll, accounts receivable, accounts payable, and financial planning. The office manipulates the university's financial resources are efficiently managed, in compliance with policies and regulations, with involvement in forecasting, investment management, and providing financial advice to university leadership.

Hormuud University's finance office has offer tuition fees for the university to better support students during challenging times, ensuring education remains accessible to all. The study mode is flexible, allowing students to study full-time or part-time on a programme (Faculty). The fees vary from one Programme (Faculty) to another and also by the mode of study. The current fees for students are available below table.

We encourage students to reach out to our office if they require any assistance or have any questions regarding their fees. Thank you for your understanding and cooperation as we work together to create a more inclusive and supportive educational environment at Hormuud University.
