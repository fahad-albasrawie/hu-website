Quality Policy and Objectives

WE ARE COMMITTED TO PROVIDE QUALITY, EFFICIENT AND EFFECTIVE SERVICE TO THE UNIVERSITY STAKEHOLDERS

Quality Policy

Hormuud University is committed to provide quality, efficient and effective service to the University stakeholders through a dynamic and excellent management system imbued with values and professionalism.

Quality Objectives

  • Identify the needs and wants of the University stakeholders.
  • Attain organizational efficiency and effectiveness.
  • Develop and improve competencies of human resource.
  • Disseminate information efficiently to all stakeholders.
  • Ensure functional and efficient management systems.
  • Provide adequate resources and facilities.
  • Improve quality services continuously through a responsive feedback mechanism.
